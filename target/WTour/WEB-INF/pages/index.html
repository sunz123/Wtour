Little Story

Europe, the full name of "Europe", originated from the Greek mythological figure "Europa" (Greek: Ερώπης), Europe is located in the northwest of the eastern hemisphere, north of the Arctic Ocean, west of the Atlantic Ocean, south of the Mediterranean Sea and the Black Sea.

In Europe, the northernmost point is norchen in Norway, the southernmost point is marocchi in Spain, and the westernmost point is Rocca in Portugal.
